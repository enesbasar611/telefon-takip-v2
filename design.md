# Design System Documentation



This document outlines the core visual principles and tokenized values that define our product's aesthetic.



## Theme Overview



Our design system is currently set to a **Light** color mode, prioritizing clarity and a clean visual experience.



## Color Palette



The color palette is derived to create a vibrant and expressive feel, with a strong emphasis on brand identity.



* **Primary Color:** `#4C6FFF` - A bright, energetic blue used for primary calls to action, active states, and key brand elements.

* **Secondary Color:** `#10B981` - A fresh, balanced green, providing a complementary accent for secondary actions or informational elements.

* **Tertiary Color:** `#F59E0B` - A warm, inviting orange, used sparingly for highlights, warnings, or distinct accentuation.

* **Neutral Color:** `#080C14` - A deep, almost black neutral, serving as a robust background or text color in contrasting scenarios.



## Typography



Our typographic system is designed for readability and visual hierarchy across all interfaces.



* **Headline Font:** `Manrope` - Modern and clean, ideal for prominent titles and headings to convey a professional yet approachable tone.

* **Body Font:** `Inter` - Highly legible and versatile, used for most UI text, paragraphs, and general content.

* **Label Font:** `Inter` - Consistent with the body font for clarity and coherence in interactive labels, buttons, and form fields.



## Shape and Form



The visual language incorporates a moderate level of roundedness to ensure a friendly and contemporary feel.



* **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate) - UI elements feature noticeable, but not overly aggressive, rounded corners, striking a balance between softness and structure.



## Spacing



The design system employs a normal level of spacing to ensure content breathability and clear distinction between elements, without feeling overly sparse.



* **Spacing:** `2` (Normal) - Provides comfortable padding and margins, contributing to a balanced and organized layout.
